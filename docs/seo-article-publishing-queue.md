# BookAnInstructor SEO Article Publishing Queue

## Purpose

Use this as the working queue for SEO articles on `https://bookaninstructor.com`.

Each article should help a real client or instructor make a booking decision, not just define a dance style. Every page should link clearly into the app:

- Post a job: `https://app.bookaninstructor.com/post-job`
- Find an instructor: `https://app.bookaninstructor.com/instructors`
- Become an instructor: `https://app.bookaninstructor.com/instructor-signup`

## Publishing Rules

- Write in BookAnInstructor's voice: practical, clear, trusted and helpful.
- Keep each article original. Use planning notes as source ideas only.
- Avoid copied history-heavy text.
- Include the city/category naturally where relevant, especially Brisbane and Queensland during launch.
- Add one clear primary CTA near the top and one at the bottom.
- Add internal links to related category pages once those pages exist.
- Do not make claims about insurance, screening or guarantees unless the platform process supports them.
