How to Book a Ballet Teacher for Private Lessons or Workshops

Ballet bookings can range from beginner private lessons through to school workshops, studio classes, exam support and audition preparation. A clear request helps ballet teachers understand the level, goals and teaching style required.

Find a ballet teacher

Know the purpose of the lesson

Some clients want classical technique, some need exam preparation, while others want ballet for fitness, confidence or a performance. Include the reason for the booking so instructors can respond with relevant experience.

Include age, level and background

Ballet teachers need to know whether the student is a child, teen or adult, and whether they are a beginner, returning dancer or experienced student. If a syllabus or exam system matters, include that too.

Private lessons versus group classes

A one-on-one ballet lesson can be highly personalised. A school or studio class needs more planning around space, group size, music, safety and class management.

Compare more than price

Look at the instructor's profile, experience, communication and availability. A suitable ballet teacher should explain how they can support the student or group.
