Private Dance Lessons: How to Choose the Right Instructor

Private dance lessons are personal. The right instructor should match your goals, style, level, location and preferred way of learning.

Post a private lesson request

Choose the right style

Start by choosing the style you want to learn. Hip hop, ballet, jazz, tap, contemporary, wedding dance, salsa and musical theatre all require different teaching approaches.

Be honest about your level

Beginners, returning dancers and advanced students need different support. Let instructors know your current level so they can suggest the right lesson structure.

Share your goal

Your goal might be confidence, fitness, technique, a wedding dance, audition preparation or simply trying something new. A clear goal helps instructors tailor the session.

Compare replies carefully

Look at the instructor's experience, availability, price and communication style. The best choice is often the person who understands your request and explains how they can help.

Use reviews for confidence

Reviews from completed bookings help you understand how an instructor works with real clients. Look for signs of professionalism, reliability, clear communication and whether clients would book again.
