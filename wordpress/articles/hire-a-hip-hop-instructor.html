Where hip hop instructors are commonly booked

Schools often book hip hop teachers for incursions, cultural programs, end-of-term activities and performance preparation. Studios may need a teacher for cover classes, workshops or choreography. Private clients may want one-on-one lessons for confidence, fitness or a special event.

Match the level of the group

A beginner group needs a different approach to an advanced crew or studio class. Include the age group, experience level and whether the session should be technique-based, choreography-based or more recreational.

Plan school workshops clearly

For school bookings, include the number of students, year level, class length, location, available space and any requirements around music or learning outcomes.

Use replies to compare fit

A strong instructor reply should confirm availability, relevant hip hop experience, proposed price and a short explanation of how they would approach the class.
