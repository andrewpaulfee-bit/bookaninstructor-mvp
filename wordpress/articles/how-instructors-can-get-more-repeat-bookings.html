How Instructors Can Get More Repeat Bookings

Repeat bookings come from trust. Clients are more likely to book again when the first session is organised, professional and easy to manage from start to finish.

Create or update your instructor profile

Deliver a reliable first booking

Arrive prepared, understand the booking details and adapt to the group. A strong first experience makes it easier for clients to choose you again.

Communicate clearly before and after class

Keep messages practical and professional. Confirm details before the session and mark the class completed once it is done.

Ask for reviews through the platform

Reviews help show future clients what you are like to work with. Completed bookings and reviews build confidence over time.

Keep your profile accurate

Update your service areas, styles and availability so the right clients can find you.

Use booking records for ongoing work
